# Tokens
The smallest individual unit in a program is known as a *token* or a *lexical Unit*
- KeyWords (if, for, each, print, class, etc)
- Literals (`x=2`) 
- Identifiers (`name`)   
- Operators (`+`, `-` ,`/` ,`*` ,`=` ,`==`)
- Punctuators (`;`, `,`, `{}`, `()`, `:`)
